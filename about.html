Who Are We

Gangstas to Growers is a grassroots organization that grew out of a need to give risk youth and formerly incarcerated individuals a chance to become productive and active members of their community. The program provides paid entrepreneurial internships for participants to give them a chance to participate in the legitimate economy. Participants will be mentored by local farmers and will also plant, harvest, and vend produce to local restaurants and at a street side farmerâ€™s market.

What We Do

By providing at-risk individuals withhin the neighborhoods of 30310 and 30314 with opportunities for self-employment, we believe we can build bonds of trust and inspire self-determination that will help counter peer pressure, and ultimately reduce crimes against people and property in the neighborhood. By providing a stipend to participants, the program is also designed to allow individuals with past criminal records, who are largely barred from jobs that would pay a livable wage, to amass seed money for their business venture.
